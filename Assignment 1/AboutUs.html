Who are we?

We are a small team located in St Lucia, Brisbane. We specialise in building custom and boutique computers. We have been in operation since 2009 and we started as a one-man-band business in a Garage. Now, we are a team of six enthusiast computer builders who are passionate about building the best computers suited to our customers.

Where are we located?

We are located in St Lucia, Brisbane.

Shipping

We do ship computer all across Australia. However, shipping costs do apply. The cost of shipping will vary with the system (especially depending on which case is used).

Looking for an even more customised experience?

Fill in our Contact Us form or the Additional Comments section on this page with these details:

If you already know what parts you want in your build. Enter them in the comments section in the contact form in Contact Us or in the Additional Comments section here.

If you are unsure and would like some more advice. Please fill in our contact us form in Contact Us. Remember to answer these questions:

  • What are you using the computer for? (e.g. Gaming/Video Editing/Business)
  • List some of the programs you'll be using on a day to day basis. (e.g. Adobe Premier)
  • What is your budget? (A rough estimate is fine.)
  • On a scale of 1-10 (ten being the most valued), How much do you value the silence of your PC versus the performance of your PC?
